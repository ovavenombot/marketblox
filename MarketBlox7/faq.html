Help Center

Common Questions

Tap any question to read the answer

1
How long does delivery take?
Most orders are delivered within 5–30 minutes after payment. Our staff is online 24/7 and will reach out in your Discord support ticket as soon as possible. During busy periods it may take a little longer, but we always prioritise fast delivery.
2
Which payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe, and cryptocurrency. You can also pay using your wallet balance if you've topped up your account. All card payments are handled by Stripe — your details are never stored on our servers.
3
Do I need a Discord account?
Yes, Discord is required. After payment, a private support ticket is opened in our Discord server and our staff delivers your items through that ticket. Make sure to join the server before completing checkout. Discord is free at discord.com.
4
Which Roblox games do you support?
We currently support Steal A Brainrot, Sailor Piece (Grand Piece Online), Blox Fruits, and Rivals. We regularly expand to new games — follow our Discord to be the first to hear when new games are added.
5
Is it safe to buy from MarketBlox?
Absolutely. Card payments use Stripe — your details are never stored on our servers. We will never ask for your Roblox password or any account credentials. Deliveries happen entirely in-game via Roblox trade. We have hundreds of satisfied customers and a trust-first approach to every order.
6
Can I get a refund?
Yes. If your items are not delivered within 24 hours of placing your order, you are entitled to a full refund. Open a ticket in our Discord server with your order ID and we'll process it promptly. We stand behind every order we take.
7
Can I buy items for a friend?
Yes! At checkout, enter your friend's Roblox username in the username field. Make sure it's spelled exactly as it appears in Roblox — this is where our staff will deliver the items. You should still link your own Discord account for the support ticket.
8
How do I track my order?
Log into your MarketBlox account to view your full order history and status updates. You'll also receive direct updates from our staff through your Discord support ticket. If anything seems delayed, message us directly in the ticket.
