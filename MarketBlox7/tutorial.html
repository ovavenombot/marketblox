Step-by-Step Guide

How to Order on MarketBlox

Follow the steps below to get your Roblox items delivered fast and safely.

Order Process

Order Steps

Follow each step carefully for a smooth experience

1
Add Items to Your Cart
Browse through our different games and add everything you want to your cart. You can mix items from multiple games in a single order — no need to checkout separately!
2
Proceed to Checkout
When you're ready, open your cart and click "Proceed to Checkout". Review your items and quantities before moving forward.
3
Enter Your Roblox Username & Link Discord
Fill in your Roblox username accurately — this is how our staff will deliver your items in-game. Link your Discord account so your support ticket is created for the right account.
4
Complete Payment
Select your preferred payment method — we support Stripe (credit/debit card) and crypto. Complete the payment through our secure checkout flow. Your data is fully encrypted.
5
Join Our Discord Server
A private support ticket will be automatically created in our Discord server after payment. Make sure you have joined the server at discord.gg/marketblox before completing your order.
6
Receive Your Items via the Ticket
Our delivery staff will reach out through your ticket and deliver your purchased items in-game via Roblox. Just follow their instructions and enjoy your items — usually within minutes!
Secure Payments
